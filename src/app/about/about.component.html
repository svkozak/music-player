About the app

Iamplayer (beta) is a web player that uses the official Apple Music API and MusicKit JS to connect to Apple Music catalog and your iCloud Music Library.

When you press 'Sign in to Apple Music' you are redirected to Apple's website to sign in using your Apple ID. This app then receives a secure token from Apple and uses it to connect to your library without knowing your Apple ID or password.

The app does not collect or store any personal information.

Additional features and updates

I started building this app as an educational/hobby project. Since Apple have released their own web player this app is no longer maintained and will be available for some time for demo purposes.
